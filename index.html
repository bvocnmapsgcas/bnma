About Our Programme

VISION

To provide adequate Networking and Mobile Application Development skills required that would incorporate specific job roles along with broad-based general education.

MISSION

To ensure the learners have adequate skills, make them work-ready, and enhance the employability of the graduate students.

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEO)

  • • PEO 1: Demonstrate the ability to adapt to a rapidly changing environment by learning and applying new skills and competencies.
  • • PEO 2: Acquire the spirit of compassion, kinship, and commitment for National Harmony.
  • • PEO 3: Progressively adopt and learn continuously through ICT modules.
  • • PEO 4: Possess awareness and skills for lifelong learning and career development.
  • • PEO 5: Incorporate the requirements of industries in a flexible manner, developing holistic and well-groomed graduates to meet the emerging needs of the Industry.

PROGRAMME OUTCOMES (PO)

  • • PO 1: Become knowledgeable in the subject of Computer Applications and apply the principles of the same to the needs of the Employer/Institution/own Business or Enterprise.
  • • PO 2: Gain Analytical skills in the field of Computer Applications.
  • • PO 3: Understand and appreciate professional ethics, community living, and Nation Building Initiatives.
  • • PO 4: Acquire knowledge of the latest networking and mobile technologies and future trends.
  • • PO 5: Synthesize principles and theories of Networking and Mobile Applications for different computing paradigms.
  • • PO 6: Develop professional skills that prepare students for employment and lifelong learning in Networking, Mobile Applications, and related fields.
  • • PO 7: Bridge the potential skill gap identified between the Institution and Industry.
  • • PO 8: Provide vertical mobility to students.

PROGRAMME SPECIFIC OUTCOMES (PSO)

  • • PSO 1: Apply the knowledge of Networking and Mobile Application Development in domains like Banking, Insurance, Health, Robotics, Environment, and Biology.
  • • PSO 2: Solve complex problems in the field of Networking and Mobile Application Development with an understanding of the societal, legal, and cultural impacts of the solution.
  • • PSO 3: Identify core networking and mobile application development concepts, and design IT infrastructure including devices, mobile applications, topologies, protocols, systems software, management, and security.
  • • PSO 4: Form a part of a team with the right attitudes and contribute effectively to projects and initiatives.

WHY BNMA?

The BNMA program, initiated in 2019, focuses on practical learning, emphasizing hands-on experience and real-world applications to develop essential skills. The curriculum includes two industrial training sessions and industry visits to enhance learning, supplemented by projects that foster practical skills and provide exposure to industry standards. Central government funding ensures quality and affordable education, making it accessible for all students. The program has successfully graduated three batches, with alumni securing careers in IT and pursuing further studies. NASSCOM assessments validate the skills acquired, enhancing employability and providing industry recognition for career advancement. Additionally, the program promotes employability skills through workshops, seminars, and exercises, preparing graduates for job readiness in the competitive market.
